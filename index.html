Blackhole

Blackhole is a web proxy debugger made for frontend development.
With it, you modify your javascipt, etc. Refresh your browser to see your code in action.
Works both on pc and your mobile devices.

Features

  • Visit your local resource across your mobile devices immediately
  • Route http requests to your local content or remote server based on configuration
  • Modify remote requests using various addons
  • script your own addon
